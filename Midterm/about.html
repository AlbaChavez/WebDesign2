About Us

Rich flavors that make your tastebuds tingle

Located in Santa Monica, California, our company, Helados La Michoacana, produces and distributes the highest quality paletas, other frozen novelties, and tasty Mexican snacks and munchies! Our products, featuring the La Michoacana brand with the iconic logo of the little girl from Michoacán, are available throughout thousands of retail locations across the United States and Canada.


Our Mission

Here in La Michoacana our mission is to bring the authentic Mexicano experience home to you and your family with our wide variety of delicious flavors.
